Get life under control.
Stky for iPhone


You’ve tried to-do lists. It starts out well: a blank slate, a sense of purpose. But one day you open it, see a list a mile long, and can’t take it.

Stky is different. Start each day with a fresh sticky note. Open your drawer and add only what you plan to do today. If things don't go as planned, don’t worry: you’ll start tomorrow with a fresh sticky.
